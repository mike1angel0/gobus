## Iteration 4 - TASK-004: Design OpenAPI spec — Passenger, Booking, Search
- Added 6 passenger-facing paths: GET /search, GET /trips/{scheduleId}, GET/POST /bookings, GET/DELETE /bookings/{id}
- Added schemas: SearchResult, SeatAvailability, TripDetail, Booking, BookingWithDetails, CreateBookingRequest, BookingStatus enum
- Search is public (no auth), bookings are authenticated with ownership enforcement (404 not 403)
- Trip detail includes seat map with isBooked availability per seat
- Computed segment pricing: price at destination minus price at origin stop
- Learnings: Used `format: date` (not `date-time`) for tripDate query params and request fields since these are date-only values. Cancel booking returns 200 with updated booking (not 204) to provide immediate feedback to the client.
